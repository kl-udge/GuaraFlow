# **GuaraFlow**: Smart Payments & Tracking for SACCOs

## Problem Statement
In many SACCOs, members face challenges such as:
- **Inefficient Tracking**: Difficulty tracking savings, loans, and other contributions.
- **Guarantor Visibility**: Lack of an easy way to find a guarantor or track the status of individuals they've guaranteed.
- **Delayed Notifications**: Members are not promptly notified when a person they've guaranteed defaults or when there are updates on contributions.
- **Limited Flexibility in Contributions**: Current systems don't allow for flexible contributions to specific items (e.g., savings, loan repayments).

## Goal
To address these issues, **GuaraFlow** aims to develop a smart payment and tracking solution that integrates **Mpesa** and **stablecoins** to:
1. **Streamline payments**: Allow members to contribute using Mpesa and stablecoins.
2. **Track all transactions**: Ensure members can easily monitor savings, loans, and repayments.
3. **Improve guarantor management**: Enable seamless identification of potential guarantors and provide visibility into the status of those you've guaranteed.
4. **Notify members**: Send timely notifications for updates, missed payments, or relevant actions related to your account or the people you've guaranteed.
5. **Support customizable contributions**: Allow members to contribute to specific items, such as savings, loan repayments, or new offerings introduced by the SACCO.

## Initial Focus: Mpesa and Stablecoin Integrations
We are starting by integrating **Mpesa** and **stablecoins** as payment options, which will enable:
- **Mpesa Integration**: Allow members to make payments directly from their mobile devices, using a familiar, secure, and widely available platform.
- **Stablecoin Integration**: Enable members to contribute using stablecoins, offering an alternative, secure, and reliable digital payment method that can help mitigate local currency fluctuations.

## Solution Components
1. **Mpesa Payment Integration**: Facilitate secure mobile payments for SACCO contributions.
2. **Stablecoin Integration**: Provide an option for members to contribute using stablecoins, enhancing financial flexibility.
3. **Notification System**: Real-time alerts for members on important events, such as default notices or successful repayments.
4. **Guarantor Dashboard**: A dashboard that provides a clear overview of your guarantees and their status.
5. **Customizable Offerings**: Allow SACCOs to add or remove specific items for contributions, such as special savings accounts, emergency funds, or investment products.

## Next Steps
- Build and test the **Mpesa** payment integration.
- Develop and integrate **stablecoin** contributions into the system.
- Implement a notification system with customizable triggers.
- Design a user-friendly dashboard for guarantor tracking and member contributions.
